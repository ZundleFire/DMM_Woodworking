Our Story

Craftsmanship from start to finish.

DMM Woodworking LLC was built on a simple belief: every home deserves cabinetry that's truly made for it. We design and build every piece completely from scratch — no stock cabinets, no shortcuts.

Based in Lancaster, PA, we work directly with homeowners, contractors and designers throughout the region to bring custom woodworking projects to life — on time and to the highest standard.

Whether it's a full kitchen renovation, a custom bathroom vanity or a built-in closet system, we bring the same skill and care to every project, large or small.
